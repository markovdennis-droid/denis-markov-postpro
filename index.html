About

Ten years in
film post-production.

My name is Denis Markov. I work and live in Spain. I have been working as a post-production producer for ten years, with deep knowledge and extensive experience in film post-production.

In 2019, I organized a freelance community of talented and reliable professionals with experience in film production: editors, sound engineers, colorists, DI supervisors.

We work with all video and audio formats and are able to solve any technical and creative tasks for our clients of any size.

"We make what you see in your head real. Just a little better."

We went remote during the last global pandemic, and despite the challenges, successfully released several projects in film, advertising, and TV shows. We currently specialize in remote post-production.

— Denis Markov
